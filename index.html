What It Does
A smarter way to manage
PSU research

The Research Atlas addresses the difficulty of organizing and analyzing faculty research outputs across PSU campuses.

Centralized Repository

All faculty research metadata — titles, abstracts, campus, year, discipline — organized in one searchable platform.

AI-Assisted Classification

Automatically extracts and groups research themes from titles and abstracts using large language model classification.

Thematic Gap Analysis

Highlights recurring, emerging, and underrepresented research areas to support institutional research planning.

Research Timeline

Track research output trends over time and per campus to identify growth areas and stagnation across departments.

AI-Powered
Instant theme classification

Paste any research title or abstract and the system will classify it into a PSU thematic cluster, extract keywords, and assess confidence — instantly.
